HOW TO EAT A CBC BURRITO

Not to worry; we’ve got you (and your burrito) covered. The foil doesn’t just keep your burrito warm. If unwrapped correctly, it also aids the tortilla and keeps everything in its place.
Yes, the foil serves a dual purpose. And it’s pretty easy once you get the hang of it.

All you have to do is peel back the foil in small strips. The foil holds everything together. Pretty neat, right?
